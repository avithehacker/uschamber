Students/entrepreneurs often have a very “silo-based functional” view of startups situations and lack the understanding of legal and accounting related compliances throughout their journey, which can be resolved through legal expertise but, how?? With this COVID-19 pandemic upon us.


SEnPik (by Cleverso Skills Pvt. Ltd.) with US Law Chambers LLP and in association with AIC - Mahamana Foundation for Innovation and Entrepreneurship - IM-BHU (AIC-MFIE-IM-BHU), Institute of Management Studies, Banaras Hindu University, Varanasi (AIC-MFIE-IM-BHU is supported and funded by NITI Aayog, Govt. of India) started a webinar series on Legal Understanding and Compliances for Start-Ups. This informative-cum-interaction series and its first-ever session will be held on 17th April 2020, through online cloud video conferencing.


This new lecture series would bring Entrepreneurs, Startups & Research individuals to get the insight of Legal solutions to the problem to occur during their entrepreneurial lifecycle. In addition, the series seeks to enhance student, entrepreneur, aspiring entrepreneurs to understand the various topics on advancement in legal and accounting released by Government time to time, it would also provide the basics and background necessary to pursue entrepreneurship as a career path. This virtual venue is also a meeting point for all the like-minded people of the region.

The topic of the first lecture of this series is “Startup Governance - understanding the unique combination of governance issues in venture-backed startups over their life cycles ” by "Mr. Sagar Anand (Advocate, Supreme Court of India) & Mr. Paritosh Tripathi (Chief Executive Officer, AIC-MFIE-IM-BHU) ".
